About The Artist

Kanye West was born on June 8, 1977, in Atlanta but raised in a middle-class household in South Shore, Chicago. With a net worth of $240 million (2019) Kanye West is one of the most influential people of this time, exploring many creative mediums as an artistic outlet.

Perhaps one of the most notable celebrities in contemporary culture, Kanye West is a producer, rapper, songwriter, record executive and fashion influencer who rose to prominence after producing Jay Z’s influential album The Blueprint in 2001. After suffering a major car accident in 2002, West turned to rapping as a manner with which to recover; amassing in the release of College Dropout (in 2004) and Late Registration (2005) that kickstarted his career as a rapping sensation and led to many more critically acclaimed albums over the years.

Kanye has never been shy of controversy either, including comments about American politics and live interruptions at awards ceremonies. It is safe to say that Kanye West’s entrepreneurial, political and creative legacy is distinctive, stating him as legendary within multiple industries.

The rapper and record producer has released 137 singles, 4 promo singles and has similarly charted with 65 other songs as features. West has sold 100 million digital downloads worldwide, and 32.7 million albums. By 2016, he achieved the tenth most top-40 hit records by a male artist in Billboard’s near-70 year history that amassed a total of forty visits to the top-40 charts as a lead and a featured artist.
